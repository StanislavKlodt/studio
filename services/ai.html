FAQ

  • Will AI cost us monthly like a subscription?

    It depends. If you're using third-party infrastructure (OpenAI, Google Cloud, Amazon Bedrock), you’ll pay based on usage — queries, compute, data storage. However, cloud isn't always cheaper. Sometimes, training your own model and running it in-house is more cost-effective. We compare both paths — cost, risk, scalability — and recommend the one that works best for your business

  • What data is needed for AI models to work?

    Depends on the goal. For analytics and forecasting — historical data (sales, customers, inventory, transactions). For chatbots — conversation logs, user queries, internal guidelines. Structured and labeled data is best, but we can work with raw datasets too If data is limited or scattered, we help organize, collect, and enhance it using similar datasets. We don’t confuse you with jargon — just tell you what’s needed, what’s possible, and how to get results

  • Can non-tech teams use AI tools?

    Yes. All our solutions are designed for regular users — no machine learning expertise required. Interfaces are familiar — chats, CRMs, dashboards. Business logic is built-in Before launch, we train your team, provide instructions, and offer ongoing support. The system should help, not require extra specialists

  • Can you trust AI-generated content like text, images, or videos?

    AI can quickly create solid drafts — content, visuals, ideas. But the best results come when humans refine them. We help you build a workflow where AI generates first drafts, offers ideas, and handles repetitive tasks — and your team polishes the result. It’s efficient, cost-effective, and quality-driven

  • How does AI improve data handling and reporting?

    AI automates data collection, structuring, and analysis — revealing trends and anomalies. In AI-powered BI systems, dashboards update in real time, and algorithms uncover hidden connections to support smarter decisions

  • Is it secure? What about GDPR and privacy?

    Yes — we follow GDPR, ISO, and other industry standards. We use encryption, secure channels, and access controls. If needed, we deploy isolated solutions and prepare full legal documentation for data protection We work with EU businesses and ensure full compliance. Your data is safe with us
