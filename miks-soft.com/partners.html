FAQ

When will I get paid?

We process payouts within 15 business days after the client pays their invoice

Do I have to register for the partnership program?

If you have a client right now — you can share their contact without registration. But if you plan to recommend leads regularly and earn commissions, it’s better to register. This way, we can respond faster and support you throughout the process

What if the deal takes a long time to close?

B2B sales can take time — and we get that. We secure the lead under your name for three invoices. No matter how long it takes for a client to pay — you still get your part

What if my client needs a service not listed on the website?

Message our partnership manager and explain the task — chances are, we can handle it. We cover the full development cycle, and not everything is listed on the site

Is there a guarantee that I’ll get my money?

Absolutely. We sign a contract outlining all the terms. If you’re self-employed or a company, you send us an invoice for consulting services. If you’re an individual — we’ll transfer the money via a convenient method

What if you say the client didn’t pay?

We’re here for the long run and we value our partners. Destroying trust means destroying the business. Every lead you bring is logged in our CRM — we notify you the moment the client pays

How do I hand over a client?

It’s super easy: message us by WhatsApp, email, or submit a request on the website. We’ll contact you to discuss the details
